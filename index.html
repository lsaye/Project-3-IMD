Whack A Zombie!

A pixel-art arcade game inspired by Whack a Mole where you test your reflexes by whacking the zombies who try to leave their graves!

How To Play!

  • Objective: Click/tap on the Zombies as they pop out of the nine gravestones to earn points.
  • Scoring: Each successful click on a Zombie increases your score.
  • Caution: If a Bomb appears, do not click on it! Clicking the bomb will immediately end your game.
  • Goal: Score as many points as you can!
